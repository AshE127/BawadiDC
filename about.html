Who We Are

A Full-Service Halal Restaurant Rooted in Tradition

Since opening our doors in 2018, Bawadi Mediterranean Grill has been a gathering place for our community in Northern Virginia. We are a full-service, fully Halal restaurant serving authentic Mediterranean and Middle Eastern food — from the freshest hummus and smoky kabobs to the rich, slow-cooked Mansaf that brings the taste of the Levant right to your table.

Our menu draws from family recipes passed down through generations. These dishes have been refined over the years, but the soul — and the taste — remains true to its authentic heritage. Whether you're dining in for a family meal, enjoying our open buffet, or ordering catering for a special occasion, you'll find food made with real ingredients and genuine care.

Our Food

Authentic Ingredients, Authentic Flavors

We serve breakfast on Saturdays and Sundays, lunch and dinner seven days a week. Our menu spans appetizers and mezze, grilled meats, Palestinian daily specials, fresh seafood, homemade soups, and traditional sweets. Everything is prepared fresh each day.

You'll find dishes like Mansaf — the beloved Jordanian/Palestinian rice and lamb dish — alongside Musakhan, Qidra, Kabsa, and a full selection of kabobs and shawarma. Our vegetarian options include housemade falafel, baba ghannouj, grape leaves, and a full mezze spread.

Not sure what to order? Our staff will happily guide you through the menu — that's what we're here for.
