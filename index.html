Our Story

A Taste of Home, Right Here in Virginia

Since 2018, Bawadi Mediterranean Grill has brought the authentic flavors of the Mediterranean and Palestinian tradition to Falls Church. Every dish is prepared from family recipes handed down through generations — from our smoky kabobs and creamy hummus to the rich, slow-cooked Mansaf and crispy falafel.

We're proud to be one of the leading halal Mediterranean restaurants in Northern Virginia, serving our community with honesty, hospitality, and food showered with love.
