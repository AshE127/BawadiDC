For the Restaurant

How to Post Updates Yourself

Three easy ways to keep your customers informed — no developer needed, no technical skills required.

📲
FreeEasiest

Post on Facebook

1
Open Facebook on your phone or computer.
2
Go to your Bawadi Facebook page.
3
Click "Create Post" — type your special, add a photo, and post.
4
Your followers see it instantly. This site links to your page.

Best for: daily specials, photos of new dishes, event announcements, holiday hours

📍
FreeGreat for SEO

Post on Google Business

1
Go to business.google.com and sign in.
2
Select your Bawadi listing.
3
Click "Add update" or "Offer" — write your special and add a photo.
4
It appears on your Google Maps listing for all searchers to see.

Best for: updating hours, posting offers, improving your search ranking, responding to reviews

📧
EasyDirect to Customers

Email or WhatsApp List

1
Collect emails via the form above (they go straight to your inbox).
2
Use Mailchimp (free up to 500 contacts) to send a simple email.
3
Or use a WhatsApp Broadcast list — message loyal customers directly.
4
Announce specials, Ramadan programs, new menu items, and events.

Best for: loyal customers, Ramadan programs, big event announcements, exclusive offers

⭐ Our Recommendation

The easiest system that requires zero technical knowledge: post on Facebook every time you have a special, and update your Google Business hours whenever they change. That's it. Your customers will find you, and this website will always link them to your latest Facebook posts.
